GAME DESCRIPTION

Planets Merge: Puzzle Games takes you on an intergalactic puzzle adventure, where you combine planets to create celestial wonders.

Drag, align, and merge planets to form bigger, more powerful celestial bodies while exploring the mysteries of the universe. The simple yet addictive gameplay will keep you entertained as you unlock new galaxies, rare planets, and cosmic surprises.

Are you ready to craft your own universe and witness the birth of celestial marvels?
